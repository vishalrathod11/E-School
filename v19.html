Easy-to-use Mobile App

Schoop, the fully compatible Android and iOS Mobile App is designed to address the modern challenging environment of the management, parents, teachers etc. This App enables the parents to monitor the activities and performances of the students with ease and promotes efficient parent-teacher interaction.

Mobile App for Admin

Stabilize and manage school administration procedures of all kinds without any hassles

Mobile App for Principal

Has all the privileges of admin and can monitor and access data of the entire School

Mobile app for Parents

Enables parents to access all information pertaining to their wards

Mobile app for Students

Allows students to proceed and access their academics in a smooth and systematic manner

Mobile app for Management

The management can either check or encourage the activities of the other users at anytime

Mobile App for Teachers

Access to the complete profile of each student with privileges to enter assignments, academic scores, attendance etc.
