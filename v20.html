About e-School

eSchool is a cloud based School Management and Learning Management System that makes managing educational institutions easy and efficient. It provides useful insights for students, teachers, administrators and parents and helps them make better and faster decisions.

eSchool is very easy and intuitive to use. Anyone with basic skills of using a computer or mobile phone would find it easy to setup and use eSchool. We offer a 30-day FREE trial for our paid plans (Basic, Medium, Large).
